Order Rush!

Visit Philly and try some of the local favorites!

You'll be given a random order total at the start of the game.
Click on one of the four menu items below, and a specific amount will be added to your order total.

Win your order for free by matching the random order total with your own order total!

Go over the total and be prepared to fork over some cash because, you've lost!!

The value of each menu item is hidden until you click, and the value will change with each new game.
